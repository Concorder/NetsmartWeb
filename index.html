Mobile payment methods

Mobile payments are an increasingly attractive way to pay because they’re secure, fast, and convenient.

In fact, end users don’t have to register or provide personal data; they just need to send an SMS and the transaction is completed.

We offer mobile billing through Premium SMS (PSMS), Direct Operator Billing, Online Billing Services (OBS) and In-App Billing that offers high conversion rates, providing “Single-Click” payments where available.

Accepting payments by mobile means exposure to potential billions of mobile users worldwide, instantly.

Netsmart mobile payment methods include:

Premium SMS

Carrier Billing (DCB)

Premium SMS

Mobile payments through Premium SMS (MO/MT) is one of today’s most popular micro payment channels. It is one of the simplest and most widely available payment methods, because all it takes is to send and receive an SMS message – a feature supported by every mobile phone.

Customers send a text message to a premium-rated SMS short code.

The purchase amount is charged to their mobile phone bill.

Benefits of
Premium SMS

Global reach

High conversion rates compared to credit cards – no long form fills needed.

Fast and easy activation with a dedicated SMS Short code

Available for both one-time purchases and subscriptions

Carrier Billing (DCB)

Also known as Direct Mobile Billing, Direct To Bill and Direct Operator Billing is accessible to any smartphone owner having a subscription or a prepaid account with a mobile operator.

The transaction is entirely secured and does not require any personal or banking information.

The mobile user validates the payment with one click and has immediate access to service.

Then purchase is then added on mobile bill or prepaid account, without any action on user’s part, nor additional costs.

Benefits of
Carrier Billing

Access to new users

Simple and fast checkout

Flexible charging frequency and price

Available for both one-time purchases and subscriptions

NetSmart now also offers Online Billing Services (OBS), a variation of a direct billing service.

OBS is now made available cross carrier in South Africa. A unique feature of South Africa’s OBS is that it provides a ‘silent billing’ model for subscription services.
